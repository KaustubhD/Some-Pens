What is this ?

The following is a list of some phobias that are found to be
more common than others.
Some of the images can be fear inducing, so choose between
Graphical (With all images) or Non Graphical.

Vertigo

The fear of heights.
A common phobia among individuals.

Batophobia

The fear of deep waters.
Another common phobia among individuals.(Mostly mothers)

Coulrophobia

The fear of clowns.
A phobia considered childish.

Haemophobia

The fear of blood.
A phobia which can render its victims catatonic even at a sight of blood.

Pediophobia

The fear of dolls.
A phobia which brings back fond memories of little Annie.
See the Credits section

Trypophobia

The fear of objects with numerous holes.
An uncommon one. Very easy to acquire with the right stimulus.
